✈️ 落地入境 ✈️ Arrival & Immigration

飞机着陆后，你需要依次通过以下8个环节。提前了解每个环节的要求，可以大幅缩短通关时间。

After landing, you'll go through the following steps. Knowing what to expect can significantly speed up the process.

🛂

2.1 机场通关流程（8步） 2.1 Airport Immigration Flow (8 Steps)

  1. 🩺 健康检疫Health Quarantine
    如有发热、咳嗽等症状需主动申报。来自黄热病疫区需出示疫苗接种证书。 Declare if you have fever/cough. Yellow fever vaccination certificate required if coming from endemic areas.
  2. 🖐️ 指纹采集Fingerprint Collection
    14岁以下或70岁以上免采。短期签证申请人至2026年底亦免采指纹。 Exempt: under 14, over 70, or short-term visa applicants (through Dec 2026).
  3. 🛂 边防检查Border Inspection
    出示护照+入境卡二维码（或纸质入境卡）。边检官可能询问：来华目的、停留时间、住宿地址。 Present passport + arrival card QR code (or paper card). Officer may ask: purpose, duration, accommodation address.
  4. 🧳 行李提取Baggage Claim
    查看航班对应的行李转盘号。 Check the baggage carousel number for your flight.
  5. 🛃 海关申报Customs Declaration
    无申报物品走绿色通道；有需申报物品（超额现金、贵重物品等）走红色通道。 Green channel if nothing to declare; red channel if carrying cash over limit, valuables, etc.
  6. 🚪 出口Exit
    出来后即是到达大厅，可购买本地SIM卡、兑换人民币、打车或乘地铁。 Arrival hall: buy local SIM, exchange currency, take taxi or metro.

3.1 移动支付设置（最重要！） 3.1 Mobile Payment Setup (Most Important!)

中国几乎已进入"无现金社会"。街头摊贩、出租车、甚至寺庙功德箱都使用二维码支付。出发前完成下列设置，是在华旅行顺畅的最关键一步。

China is nearly cashless. Street vendors, taxis, even temple donation boxes use QR code payments. Setting this up before departure is the single most important step for a smooth trip.

  1. 下载 Alipay — App Store / Google Play 搜索 "Alipay"，注意不是支付宝国内版
  2. Download Alipay — Search "Alipay" in App Store / Google Play
  3. 注册账号 — 使用你的手机号（支持国际号码）注册
  4. Sign up — Use your phone number (international numbers supported)
  5. 实名认证 — 上传护照照片进行身份验证
  6. Verify identity — Upload passport photo for verification
  7. 绑定外卡 — 进入"我的"→"银行卡"→"添加银行卡"，支持 Visa / Mastercard / JCB / Amex
  8. Link card — Go to "Me" → "Bank Cards" → "Add Card", supports Visa / Mastercard / JCB / Amex
  9. 开始使用 — 扫描商家二维码或出示付款码即可。单笔200元以下免手续费。
  10. Start using — Scan the merchant's QR code or show your payment code. No fee for transactions under ¥200.
  1. 下载 WeChat — 国际版微信，注册账号
  2. Download WeChat — International version, sign up
  3. 进入支付 — "我"→"服务"→"钱包"→"银行卡"
  4. Go to Pay — "Me" → "Services" → "Wallet" → "Bank Cards"

  6. Link card — Supports Visa / Mastercard / JCB. Note: WeChat has lower per-transaction limits for foreign cards than Alipay.

4.2 使领馆信息 4.2 Consulates & Embassies

👋 离境指南 👋 Departure Guide

离开中国前，退税、免税购物、离境流程——最后一步也要稳稳当当。

Tax refunds, duty-free shopping, departure procedures — finish your journey smoothly.

💰

5.1 离境退税流程 5.1 Departure Tax Refund

条件：

Eligibility:

  • 在同一退税商店同一天消费满 ¥500
  • Spend ≥¥500 in one day at a single tax-free store
  • 商品在购买后 90天内 携带离境
  • Take the goods out of China within 90 days of purchase
  • 离境时商品未使用、未拆封
  • Goods must be unused and unopened at departure

流程：Process:

  1. 购物时索要《离境退税申请单》和发票
  2. Ask for the "Tax Refund Application" and receipt when shopping
  3. 离境时在机场海关退税柜台出示：护照、退税单、发票、商品
  4. At airport, present at customs refund counter: passport, refund form, receipts, goods
  5. 退税率 11%（实际到手约 9%，扣除手续费）
  6. Refund rate: 11% (approx 9% after processing fee)
✈️

5.2 离境前检查清单 5.2 Final Departure Checklist

  • 确认航班时间 — 国际航班建议提前 2-3 小时到达机场
  • Confirm flight time — Arrive 2-3 hours early for international flights
  • 退税物品随身携带 — 不要托运，海关可能查验
  • Keep tax-refund items in carry-on — Customs may inspect them
  • 花完人民币 — 机场免税店是花掉剩余人民币的好地方
  • Spend remaining RMB — Airport duty-free shops are great for using leftover cash
  • 备份照片 — 确保旅行照片已备份，中国境内部分云服务（如 Google Photos）可能无法访问
  • Backup photos — Ensure photos are backed up; some cloud services (e.g. Google Photos) may be inaccessible in China
