Mobile Payment

Kicker

China is largely cashless — mobile payment is the default, accounting for over 86% of consumer transactions. Foreign visitors can now link Visa, Mastercard, JCB, and Amex directly to Alipay and WeChat Pay. No Chinese bank account needed.

86%cashless transactions
≤¥200no fee per transaction
2apps cover 95%+ of scenarios
💡 BackupCarry ¥500-1,000 RMB cash as backup. ATMs at Bank of China / ICBC / China Merchants Bank accept foreign cards (look for Visa/MC logo). If one app fails, try the other.

Foreign employees must enroll in all five social insurances within 30 days of work permit issuance. Most expats also buy supplementary international health insurance for private clinics with English-speaking doctors.

Drive + School

Drive & Learn

Foreign licenses aren't valid in China — but conversion is straightforward: temporary permit, direct swap, or written test. For families, international schools offer US/UK/IB curricula. Plan 6-12 months ahead.
