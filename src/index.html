Mobile Payment

China is cashless. Link foreign card before — most critical step.

China is largely cashless — mobile payment is the default, accounting for over 86% of consumer transactions. Foreign visitors can now link Visa, Mastercard, JCB, and Amex directly to Alipay and WeChat Pay. No Chinese bank account needed.

💡 BackupCarry ¥500-1,000 RMB cash as backup. ATMs at Bank of China / ICBC / China Merchants Bank accept foreign cards (look for Visa/MC logo). If one app fails, try the other.

Status Transfer

Already in China. Convert to long-term.

Path A

Tourist→Work

Employer→WP→exit&re-enter Z visa(or in-country per local Exit-Entry Bureau).

Path B

Student→Work

RP in-country with WP after graduation(no exit). For fresh Chinese university grads.

Path C

K Visa(STEM)

Oct 2025. STEM degree+<~40→no employer. Apply in-country. Work/research/startup.

Work & Residence

Two steps. Within 30 days of entry.

Step 1

Work Permit

Employer online. Need:degree+background check+health+contract. 15-20 biz days.

Step 2

Residence Permit

After WP→Exit-Entry Bureau. Bring:passport+WP+registration+photo. 1-5yr.
